Mount Olympus

Mount Olympus is the mythical home of the Greek gods, including Zeus, Hera, and Poseidon. Standing at 2,918 meters, it’s the highest mountain in Greece and has been a symbol of divine power since ancient times. Its unique biodiversity makes it a national park with rare plants and animals. Hikers love its mystical vibe and cloud-covered peaks — many say it still feels like the gods are watching! ⚡👑

Interesting fact :

Fact: In Greek mythology, Mount Olympus is literally the home of the gods — like Zeus, Hera, Apollo — who ruled the universe from its summit! ⚡🏛️
