Mount Ararat

Mount Ararat is a dormant volcano and the highest peak in Turkey. According to legend, Noah’s Ark came to rest on this mountain after the great flood, making it a sacred site in many religions.

Although it lies in Turkey, it is deeply symbolic for the Armenian people and features on the Armenian national emblem. It’s also known for its double peaks — Greater Ararat and Little Ararat — both snow-capped year-round

Interesting fact :

According to the Bible, Noah’s Ark came to rest on Mount Ararat after the Great Flood — making it one of the most legendary mountains in religious history.
